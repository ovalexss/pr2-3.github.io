WHY TO INVEST IN CYPRUS

PAM Consulting services include assistance and consulting when it comes to your investing plans in Cyprus. We will prioritize your needs and expectations by offering you a variety of investment options for you to choose from.

The main reasons why more and more people are investing in Cyprus are:

  • • Cyprus has the lowest corporate tax rate in Europe at 12.5%
  • • No capital gains tax or inheritance tax
  • • Tax exemptions on worldwide dividends and interest income
  • • No Immovable Property Tax
  • • Corporate tax relief up to 75% for holdings
  • • Competitive VAT rates ranging from 0-19%
  • • Low national healthcare tax contribution of 2.65-4.7%

Whether you're aiming to increase your financial possibilities or increase your business outlook, Cyprus is an ideal place to start.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION TO KNOW BEFORE IMMIGRATING TO CYPRUS

As you are planning to achieve a fresh start in Cyprus, we would like to provide you with some basic information about the island and what to expect when you arrive.

  • Capital City: Nicosia is the largest city with over 200,000 residents, meaning about 16.6% of the country's population.
  • Currency: The currency of Cyprus is the Euro.
  • Population: Cyprus' current population is 1.207 million.
  • Official Languages: Greek and Turkish, with English widely spoken.
  • Cost of Living: Compared to other EU countries, the cost of living is relatively low.
  • Job Market: Cyprus offers a wide range of markets, including finance, IT, and tourism.
  • Education: Public and private education in Cyprus meets high standards.

Our team at PAM Consulting is here to assist you with any additional information you may require.
